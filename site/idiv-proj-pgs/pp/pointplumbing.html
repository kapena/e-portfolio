Point Plumbing

Website Personalization

Point Plumbing began servicing Oahu in 1992 and has worked its way to becoming one of Honolulu's top-rated professional plumbing services. In 2018 Point Plumbing realized that they needed to create a personal website that would convert prospects into customers through an engaging online experience.

View Website

Service Request Case Study

The Plan

In planning Point Plumbing's website, we conducted research on why Point Plumbing's long-term reacquiring customers keep using Point Plumbing. We discovered that customers who frequently use Point Plumbing's services like how Point Plumbing can quickly diagnose and fix plumbing issues all in one visit.

Point Plumbing wanted to integrate a "straight to the point" service request solution for the website that allows viewers who are experiencing plumbing problems to book plumbing appointments in just two simple steps.

The Challenge

Today, an online presence is everything. Point Plumbing needed to create a unique and rememberable website identity that would convert prospects viewers into customers, increase sales and instill trust.

The Process

I'm sure you've witnessed a plumbing leak, it's a nerve-racking experience. The fundamental importance in the design of the home page was to accommodate for viewers experiencing plumbing problems and providing them a hassle-free method to get in touch with Point Plumbing immediately. By either calling (on mobile) for booking an appointment online (desktop).

1. Designed with solving customers plumbing problems in mind.

A customer is desperately searching for a plumber online. They arrive on Point Plumbing's home page, and the first thing they should see is the call to action “Have a plumbing problem?”. The customer clicks get the get in touch button.

2. An effective booking process that is simple and hassel free.

The customer is taken directly to the contact page where they can easily schedule a plumbing appointment by filling out the contact form. Once the contact form is submitted Point Plumbing is notified through email.

Design Solution

The expedited service request process that viewers are experiencing when they enter point plumbings website is solving plumbing problems and helping to drive sales directly through point plumbing's site 24/7.

80% of viewers that submit their plumbing problems become customers after there the first experience using point plumbing. Customers have expressed that the site provides a trusting sense of expertise that Point Plumbing has what it takes to be one of Hawaii's top plumbers.
