Urban Revelation

Brand Development & Brand Identity

Urban Revelation wanted to establish there brand identity by exhibiting what they do best, designing modern living spaces. Aloha pixel designed Urban Revelation's logo and company profile bookelet.

Concept

The concept for the logo and company profile booklet encompasses the use of clean, modern typography with a combination of perpendicular lines that represent planning, construction, and design.

Design

In designing the logo and company profile booklet, Aloha Pixel embodied a universal message that represented Urban Revelation's clean, simplistic modern approach to renovating and developing luxury homes.

Logo Finalized

Company Profile Booklet

Outcome

Urban Revelation's brand message is clear and clean. They make it clear through effective branding that they are a design-centric business that stands out in the building industry for there quality work in designing luxury homes. The final design of the logo and company profile presents Urban Revelation as a modern construction company that places a unique focus on design.
