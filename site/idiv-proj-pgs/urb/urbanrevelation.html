Urban Revelation

Graphic Design

Urban Revelation was a company branding project that incorporated Logo, Booklet, and Stationary Design. Urban Revelation refurbishes old buildings and transforms them into fresh, functional, and modern urban living spaces. When I created the logo for Urban Revelation, I followed the core concept behind the company's mission of bringing modern esthetics to old buildings.
