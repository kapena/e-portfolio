Urban Revelation

Brand Development & Brand Identity

Urban Revelation wanted to establish there brand identity by exhibiting what they do best, designing modern living spaces. Aloha pixel designed Urban Revelation's logo and company profile bookelet.

Concept

In the concept development stage of designing the logo and company profile booklet, I set a particular focus on using modern typography with a combination of a two-color design that incorporated the use of perpendicular lines to represent design and construction.

Design

In designing the logo and company profile bookelt, I tried to develop a universal message that represented Urban Revelation's clean, simplistic modern approach to renovating and designing luxury homes.

Result

Urban Revelation's brand message is clear to their customers that they are a design-centric company that stands out in the building industry for there quality work in designing luxury homes. The final design of the logo and company profile presents Urban Revelation as a modern construction company that places a unique focus on design.
