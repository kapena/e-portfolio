Secret of Dreaming

Digital Animation

The Secret of Dreaming is a short digital animation that tells the story of how the Dreaming spirit passed Creation on throughout the continent of Australia. By digitally recreating Jim Poulter’s children’s book, The Secret of Dreaming,  I was able to honor The Dreaming's major significance to Australian native culture.

The six images you see below depict the characters of The Secret of Dreaming in chronological order. I developed each character by hand and uploaded the drawings into Photoshop for clipping and enhancements. Barramundi, Currikee, and Bunjil were animated in Maya 3D Software.
