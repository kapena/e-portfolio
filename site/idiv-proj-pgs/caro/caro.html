Caroline Rideout

Web Design & Development

Caroline Rideout is a professional writer and artist who has lived and worked in Portland, Brooklyn and San Diego. Caroline now lives in Honolulu and works in the mediums of watercolor and oil. In her art, she seeks to capture the immaterial forces— light and wind—that create color, movement, and shape across Hawaiian landscapes. I desinged and built Caroline a clean web portfolio to display and permote her beautiful paintings.
