Hawaii Invitational of Polo

Sponsorship Kit

The Hawaii Invitational of Polo is a world-class polo event that is put on every year by Hawaii Polo Life at the Waimanalo Polo Fields on the island of Oahu. Aloha Pixel produced the logo and sponsorship marketing kit for The Hawaii Invitational of Polo event.

View Entire Kit

Concept

Appealing to luxury brands and high profile sponsors such as Cartier and Ferrari was the critical concept around the sponsor kit. The core concept of obtaining sponsors was in showcasing a word class polo event in Hawaii that can't be compared.

Design

The design needed to put Hawaii on the Map of world-class polo events. To obtain sponsors, Aloha Pixel focused on creating a design that would represent an event that revolved around polo, fashion, good music, luxury brands, and local vendors.

Outcome

The Hawaii Invitational of Polo sponsor kit established sponsorship from big brands such as The Kahala Hotel and Resort, Cartier, Ferrari and, Lamborghini. The impression of this luxury event was set correctly with a design that made a successful impact on bringing word class Polo to Hawaii.
