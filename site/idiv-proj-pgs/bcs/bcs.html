Brewer Chapman Surfboards

Web Design

The legacy of Brewer Chapman Surfboards is a valuable part of surfing history. Owl Chapman has been shaping surfboards since 70s. Owl learned to master the art of shaping surfboards for the challenging waves of Hawaii under the mentorship from father of the modern day surfboard, Dick Brewer. Owl gave me the opportunity to provide Brewer Chapman Surfboards an online stage to promote and present the rich history of his craftsman ship of authentically hand shaped surfboard collection.
