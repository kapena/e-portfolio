Brewer Chapman Surfboards

Website Personalization

Brewer Chapman Surfboards,  are hand shaped by North Shore Legend, Owl Chapman who is worldly known for being one of the principal designers of the surfboard revolution. Owl learned how to shape surfboards under his teacher Dick Brewer, hence the name Brewer Chapman Surfboards.

View Website

Concept

Before the website, Brewer Chapman Surfboards could only be ordered by directly getting in touch with Owl Chapman. In talking to Owl, I explained that he could take his surfboards shapes global with a website which would increase board sales and exposure. In planning out the site, we focused on three critical segments to communicating with customers about Brewer Chapman's history and surfboards.

Design

The first was to tell the story of how Owl Chapman learned to shape surfboards from master shaper Dick Brewer. The second segment was providing a stage to showcase each surfboard ranging from Full Guns to Semi Guns to Hot Dog boards. The final step was to incorporate an order form that allowed customers from around the world to directly inquire for a shape.

Result

The resulting message from the design of the webpage is simple, minimalistic and tell's the story of Brewer Chapman Surfboards while showcasing each board in all its beauty. Soul surfers from around the world are now finding out now that Brewer Chapman Surfboards and ordering boards from the website "It's just like magic."
