Brewer Chapman Surfboards

Website Personalization

Brewer Chapman Surfboards,  are hand shaped by North Shore Legend, Owl Chapman who is worldly known for being one of the principal designers of the surfboard revolution. Owl learned how to shape surfboards under his teacher Dick Brewer, hence the name Brewer Chapman Surfboards.

View Website

Concept

Brewer Chapman Surfboards wanted to take there surfboards shapes global. A well-designed website that showcased Brewer Chapman Surfboards would help increase board sales and exposure. In planning out the site, Aloha Pixel focused on three critical segments to communicating with customers about Brewer Chapman's history and surfboards.

Design

The first step was to tell the story of how Owl Chapman learned to shape surfboards from master shaper Dick Brewer. The second step was providing a stage to showcase each surfboard ranging from Full Guns, Semi Guns, and Hot Dog surfboards. The final step was to incorporate an order form that allowed customers from around the world to directly inquire for a shape.

Full Guns Page

Outcome

Brewer Chapman Surfboards are quality hand shaped surfboards with a personal touch that can't be copied by a machine. Just like the surfboards, the website has a unique sense of personalization. It's about soul surfing and the love for making hand shaped boards. True soul surfers from around the world are now ordering Brewer Chapman Surfboards directly from the website. "It's just like magic."
