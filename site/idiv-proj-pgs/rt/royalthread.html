Royal Thread

Branding

Royal Thread is a small in-house boutique vintage store that restores's and refurbishes vintage clothing. Royal Thread wanted to create a logo that signified vintage clothing pattern design. They wanted a logo that would attract attention to customers while combining the vintage look and feel.

Concept

A rendition of a vintage pattern that resembled vintage design trends from the 80's. Using abstract shapes and bright colors to set a visual tone and influenced the design of the logo.

Design

The typeface designed for Royal Thread was constructed entirely from the abstract shapes that were infulaced from vintage pattern design trends.

Result

The resulting logo reflects the authenticity of vintage clothing while establishing the Royal Thread brand in setting them apart from other vintage retailers.
