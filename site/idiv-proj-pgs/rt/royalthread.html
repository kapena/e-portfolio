Royal Thread

Branding

Royal Thread is a small in-house boutique vintage store that restores's and refurbishes vintage clothing. Royal Thread wanted to create a logo that signified vintage clothing design. They wanted a logo that would attract attention to customers while combining the vintage look and feel.

Concept

During the concept stage of developing Royal Thread's visual identity, Aloha Pixel researched vintage pattern designs which influenced the design of the logo. In figuring out the visual design of the logo, Aloha Pixel designed a pattern that helped establish the visual identity of the logo.

Design

Constructing the logo involved incorporating slightly modified variations of the shapes from the vintage pattern created during the concept stage.

Outcome

The resulting logo reflects the authenticity of vintage clothing and communicates to customers that Royal Thread is all about vintage apparel.
