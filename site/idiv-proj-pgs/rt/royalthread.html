Royal Thread

Graphic Design

Royal Thread was a branding design project for a vintage clothing line. When creating the logo, I was inspired by vintage patterns, shapes and colors from the 70s and 80s. I incorporated the use of abstract shapes for the typography of the logo and the color scheme derived from vintage color schemes of the 70s and 80s. The result is a playful design that reflects the youthful appeal of vintage clothing.
