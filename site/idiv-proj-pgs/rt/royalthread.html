Royal Thread

Branding

Royal Thread is a small in-house boutique vintage store that restores's and refurbishes vintage clothing. Royal Thread wanted to create a logo that signified vintage clothing design. They wanted a logo that would attract attention to customers while combining the vintage look and feel.

Concept

During the concept stage of developing Royal Thread's visual identity, Aloha Pixel researched vintage pattern designs. Aloha Pixel then created a pattern from the inspiration collected. This approach helped establish the visual direction for Royal Thread logo.

Design

Constructing the logo involved using slightly modified variations of the shapes from the vintage pattern created during the concept stage.

Outcome

The resulting logo reflects the authenticity of vintage clothing. Royal Thread's logo has a vintage look and feel to it and communicates to customers that Royal Thread is really about vintage.
