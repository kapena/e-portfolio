Royal Thread

Branding

Royal Thread was a branding design project for a vintage clothing line based in Sydney, Australia. When creating the logo, I was inspired by vintage patterns, shapes and colors from the 70s and 80s. I incorporated the use of abstract shapes for the typography of the logo and the color scheme derived from vintage color schemes of the 70s and 80s. The result is a playful design that reflects the youthful appeal of vintage clothing.

Royal Thread was was a simuated project I completed during my studies at Shillington College in Sydney, Australia where I received a Certificate IV in Design. Many thanks to all the hard working staff at Shillington who inspired and helped me.
