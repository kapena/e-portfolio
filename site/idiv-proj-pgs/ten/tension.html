Tension

Graphic Design

Tension was a event promotion for the L’oreal Melbourne Fashion Festival. I designed the Tension logo, and also created the imagery effects for the promotional poster. The logo design was inspired by meaning of tension, and the way that it emerges through the intense stare of the fashion model. By juxtaposing the logo typography with a visual sense of length, I was able to create a tense energy throughout the imagery.
