Print Is Dead

Graphic Design

Print Is Dead was a feature article in Abstract Magazine. As a design intern for Abstract Magazine, I was given the opportunity to have full control over all aspects of the design for the Print Is Dead article. This article responds to the rise of digital media, at the expense of printed material. I designed the hero illustration, layout, and infographic.

As an intern at Abstract Magazine, I worked under the mentorship of Clifford Cheng who is co-owner and designer for the internationally acclaimed Hawaii Skin Diver Magazine. Thank you Cliff for inspring and mentoring me. Karen Shishido provided the beautifully written article. A big thank you to plublisher Matthew A. Heim for generiously giving everyone who contribited to Abstract Magaize a voice.
