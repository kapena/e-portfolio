Print Is Dead

Digital Illustration & Graphic Design

Print Is Dead is was graphic design job to create an engaging digital illustration and a article layout for Abstract Magazine's "Printricacies" article. The story of Print Is Dead explains how digital printing is taking over the art of conventional printing methods today. Making it harder to find traditional printed material.

Concept

The focal point of the magazine article explains how digital printing tools are becoming the norm in publishing. This lead to the concept of the pixels resembling the use of "digital" and the fingerprint resembling the conventional printing press.

Design

The hero illustration of the fingerprint is a visual representation of how printing works with the CMYK color process. The CMYK fingerprint represents the method of traditional printing while the pixels represent the new technology of printing.

Outcome

Conventional methods eventually give birth to new digital tools. "Print" is a living process that will never disappear, no matter what manner of distribution you choose to use for the job. The design of the fingerprint represents the existence of conventional and digital printing coinciding.
