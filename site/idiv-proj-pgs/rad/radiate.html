Radiate

Print Design

Radiate was a dance performance that was put on by the Sydney Dance Company. I designed the Radiate logo and did the layout for the program booklet. When creating the logo for Radiate, I was inspired by the radiating, kinetic energy of the dancers. Which led me to skewing the letter “I” and placing it in tension with the corosponding letters. This effect deliveres a simple yet effective message about the performance.

Radiate was a simuated project I completed during my studies at Shillington College in Sydney, Australia where I received a Certificate IV in Design. Many thanks to all the hard working staff at Shillington who inspired and helped me.
