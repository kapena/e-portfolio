360 Productions

Website Personalization

360 Productions is a digital production agency in Hawaii that market's luxury real estate and brands. Aloha Pixel carefully crafted a personal website experience to showcase 360 Productions as modern digital production agency.

View Website

Concept

Five key reasons why 360 Productions made the investment to update their previous website.

360 Productions needed to modernize their previous website. The deconstruction of the home page paints a clear picture of the design flaws that 360 Productions faced.

1. Webpage Fold

Displaying content to the viewer without the need for them to scroll. It's the first thing they see, so it's critical to keep your viewers interested in your service and display content that keeps them on your website. You'll notice on the pervious site that the page is cut off and the viewer needs to scroll to find out more.

2. Navigation Links

The design of a website navigation is critical. Too many navigation links on a webpage will confuse viewers. Less is more. Condensing multiple web pages into a single page and refining your page into sections helps viewers feel less scattered when viewing your site. For example, the services page can contain the benefits of video page thus reducing the web page's navigation .

3. Logo Placement

Strategic placement of your brand's logo on your website is crucial. The logo should be centered with the navigation and scaled to a reasonable size. Currently, the logo has been placed above the navigation and oddly large. The logo in its current position takes the viewers attention away from the navigation.

4. Video Display Case

Displaying a featured video in the center of a website and surrounding it with white space on either side is a generic way to showcase an appealing video especially if it's on the home page. Modern website's that incorporate feature videos use them as full-width page backgrounds that loop and autoplay as soon as the page loads.

5. Content Planning

Effective content planning engages your viewers. When we arrive on the site, our eye's don't notice any content until we look down under the video. There is content here, but it is cut off by the page fold. Displayed content that is difficult to find is a classic example of poor content planning.

Design

The design for 360 productions is spacious, responsive, and organized. The client style guide documents the typeface, type sizing, colors and icons used throughout the entire site. By visually organizing information and putting to use effective modern web design practices, you create engagement in your online branding experience.

Home Page (Looping Video Background)

Interactive homepage with an looping video background. Creating an entirely customized experience for viewers by visually allowing them directly your brand establishes customer engagement.

Work Page

The work page features eight sections of work categories. Each work section has customized embedded video from Vimeo and tailored photo libraries. Both the video and photo features make for a genuine viewing experience within the work page.

Outcome

360 Productions newly designed website delivers an engaging online experience. By providing 360s core brand values through strategic design, you create trust within clientele and viewers online. When creating a successful website, it's about communicating with your viewer's intuitively and giving them the best viewing experience possible.
