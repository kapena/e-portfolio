Eerie 8

Graphic Design

As a design intern for Abstract Magazine's fourth issue I was given the opportunity to design the entire The Eerie 8 article. This issue of Abstract was related to the theme of scary and paranormal tales. Abstract provided me with a story about Lopaka Kapanui’s Top Oʻahu Hauntings which featured eight haunted locations on the island of Oahu. I designed everything from the hero image to the layout.
