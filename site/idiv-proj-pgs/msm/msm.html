Mustard Seed Miracle

Logo Design & Website Personalization

The Mustard Seed Miracle  is a Hawaii non-profit organization backed by the Department Of Hawaiian Homelands and other local organizations. Mustard Seed Miracle bring's together volunteers, sponsors, and donations to help construct affordable housing on the Island of Oahu.

View Website

Concept

In discussing the story of Mustard Seed Miracle, we decided to focus on hope, community and giving back. This message was critical to the designing the logo and website. As Peter Kang, VP of Mustard Seed Miracle puts it "In building a house we are rebuilding relationships. Community home building reknits social fabric".

Design

I wanted to tell a story that resembled the action of moving mountain's to improve the housing problem in Hawaii. When designing the website, I tried to make it easy for viewers to learn about MSM mission through the process of building a home with the help of volunteer's, sponsors and donations.

Logo Concepts

Result

The final delivery of the Mustard Seed Miracle brand consists of a logo and website design that communicates to viewers about what project Mustard Seed Miracle represents and how people can get involved by participating in volunteering, donating materials or giving funds to help build the next home for a family in need.

Website Design

The website for Mustard Seed Miracle is a fully responsive customized one-page site with five main sections. This one-page design take's viewers on a visual storytelling experience as they browse the website and visually learn about Mustard Seed Miracle.
