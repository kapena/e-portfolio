Vivid Live

Sydney Opera House

Vivid Live was a personal project that revolved around the Sydney Opera House and its annual Vivid Live festival. During the Vivid Live festival, The Sydeny Opera House is illuminated in various bright colors, textures, and patterns. Using digital photography, I captured an image of the Opera House in its normal state then edited it recreate the lighting of the sails in a various shades of red.

The final photo was intended for use as a promotional poster for the event. This was a simulated project.
