Tension was a promotional project that I created for the Lâ€™oreal Melbourne Fashion Festival. I designed the Tension logo, and also created the imagery effects for the promotional poster. The logo design was inspired by meaning of tension, and the way that it emerges through the intense stare of the fashion model. Tension was a print design project that I completed during my studies at Shillington College in Sydney, Australia. At Shillington College, I received a Certificate IV in design.
