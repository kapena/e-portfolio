Urban Revelation

Branding

Urban Revelation was a company branding project that incorporated Logo Design, Booklet Design, and Stationary Design. Urban Revelation refurbishes old buildings and transforms them into fresh, functional, and modern urban living spaces. When I created the logo for Urban Revelation, I followed the core concept behind the company's mission of bringing modern esthetics to old buildings.

Urban Revelation was a simuated project I completed during my studies at Shillington College in Sydney, Australia where I received a Certificate IV in Design. Many thanks to all the hard working staff at Shillington College who inspired and helped me along the way.
