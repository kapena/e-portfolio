Secret of Dreaming

Digital Animation

The Secret of Dreaming is a 2D digital animation that I created in 2012 for the Advanced Diploma of Screen and Media at RMIT University in Melbourne, Australia. This short film tells the story of how the Dreaming spirit passes creation throughout the continent of Australia. By digitally recreating Jim Poulter’s children’s book, The Secret of Dreaming,  I was able to honor The Dreaming's major significance to Australian native culture.

The six images you see below depict the characters of The Secret of Dreaming in chronological order. I developed each character by hand and uploaded the drawings into Photoshop for clipping and enhancements. Barramundi, Currikee, and Bunjil were animated in Maya 3D Software. Many thanks to the hard working staff that at The Diploma of Interactive Digital Media. Special recognitions to Greg Mcdonald for contributing to the Sound Design, Jack Charles for the Voice Over and my family for all there love and support.
