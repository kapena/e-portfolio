    Proven Design Process

    Aloha Pixel we have a proven three-step process for any design job's success whether it be Graphic, Web or Digital.

    1. Concept

    In the concept stage, we are creating a useful set of design plans.

    The process includes the following: design inspiration, branding goals, brand objectives, customer personas, and design visualization. Concepting is the first critical step to establishing a design that works for you and has visual integrity.

    2. Design

    In the design phase, Aloha Pixel is working to achieve the visual look and feel that aligns with the brand requirements outlined in the conceptual stage.

    Design creates the visual ascetic of your brand. To obtain a successful production, we need to ensure that the visual representation aligns with the brand objectives. The process includes testing variations of the design in its environment, careful color selection, style guidelines and fine-tuned attention to detail.

    3. Outcome

    The product is now ready to make an official launch.

    In the outcome stage, we pay close attention to how we can keep advancing your brand based on your customer's feedback. We work together with you to make sure that the outcome you receive is what you need for your brand. Aloha Pixel provides solutions, not just a static design. We stay with you, just like Aloha.
